Frequently asked questions

1. Watching movies is slow?

If the movie plays but stops every once in a while to wait for it to load again (despite trying to choose different servers), it needs to be determined by your device or by your network.

  • Try watching the movie on another device (computer / phone / TV...). If you change to another device, the movie will run smoothly again => because of your old device. If it is a TV, check the TV settings and disable IPv6 network connection protocol. If it's an iOS device, then try using another browser (e.g. Chrome) instead of the default Safari browser, but in general iOS players have problems with high bitrate movies + 5.1 sound.
  • If the movie runs slow on all the devices you try, with all the servers the site has to offer (VIP mode is only available), it's because international bandwidth Your network is squeezed (due to congested international lines at peak hours or broken cables...). There are 2 solutions: 1. Call to reflect with the carrier; 2. Use a VPN (virtual private network) to speed up your network. We recommend you to use the WARP application => download here.

    • Note: turning on VPN will change the route of data transmission from the server to your device, and not just turning on VPN will make the network faster - sometimes turning on VPN will make your network slower! Just like when this route is congested, traffic is congested, then you should change to another more open route, but other times it's the other way around!

      Therefore, when watching movies with lag, do the following to find the fastest data transmission route:
    • Turn off VPN then try changing each server on the site
    • Turn on VPN then try changing each server on the site
      • ...until you find a combination that makes watching movies smoother. And remember, that combination is not necessarily the best all the time. So whenever there is lag, repeat the above steps!

2. Having problems with the sound: the movie has no sound, the character's voice is lost, or the sound is cracked?

  • If viewing on mobile: The sound error is caused by your browser (usually Chrome). Install & use Firefox browser!
  • If you watch on PC: Unlike movies/clips on other websites (including Youtube), movies on WatchMovie use 5.1 sound (6 channels) instead of stereo (2 channels). If the device you are viewing has only 2 speakers, you need to set up the program manage the sound on the device properly: choose the right mode with the number of speakers you have (stereo), don't choose more, otherwise your device will try to output sound to non-existent speakers => loss of sound . For example, here is the selection of Realtek HD Audio Manager sound mode: click here

3. How can I watch movies on TV?

To watch movies on TV, your TV must have a web browser. Most Smart TVs in recent years have a built-in browser. If your TV does not have a browser available, you can install it from the app store (Google Play Store / CH Play / App Store) On tv. With Android TV, you should install Puffin browser. After installing the browser, visit the website as you would on your computer/phone and watch the movie.

If you can't watch a movie using a browser on your TV, you can connect your computer to your TV (usually via HDMI) and play it from your computer to your TV.
